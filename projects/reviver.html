Reviver

Forecast worked with Reviver to effectively redefine the way people interact with their license plate by introducing an all digital system. Forecast developed a narrative for Reviver, creating a more emotional, approachable approach through visual storytelling and brand strategy. We believe the automation of a digital license plate will enhance the driving experiencing, through solving inefficiencies, increasing awareness, and improving driver communication.

Reviver reimagines the way vehicles collect and activate data to make driving easier, safer, and more efficient. By transforming the 125-year-old license plate into a connected car platform. The Reviver rPlate enhances the driving experience from the outside, integrating seamless connectivity with exterior services and meaningful communication through localized alerts and customized messaging. After working directly with the state legislature to achieve full compliance, Reviver completed a successful pilot program in California and plans to launch in Arizona, Texas, and Florida in 2018.
